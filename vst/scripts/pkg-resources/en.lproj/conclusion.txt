Oedipa __VERSION__ — installation complete.

Oedipa is now available system-wide. Where to find it:

    Logic Pro       AU MIDI FX slot on a software-instrument track
    Bitwig Studio   Note FX slot, as VST3 or CLAP, before an instrument
    Reaper          VST3 or CLAP in any MIDI / FX chain
    Studio One      VST3 in the MIDI fx slot

Your DAW may need a plug-in rescan or restart before Oedipa appears in the browser.

Project: https://github.com/im9/oedipa
Support: https://github.com/im9/oedipa/issues

Thank you for installing Oedipa.
