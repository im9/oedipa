Welcome to the Oedipa installer.

Oedipa is a Tonnetz-based chord-exploration MIDI effect by im9. This installer places the Oedipa plug-in (VST3, AU, and CLAP formats) into the system-wide Audio Plug-Ins folders, so any DAW on this Mac can find it:

    /Library/Audio/Plug-Ins/VST3/Oedipa.vst3
    /Library/Audio/Plug-Ins/Components/Oedipa.component
    /Library/Audio/Plug-Ins/CLAP/Oedipa.clap

You will be asked for your administrator password before installation.

If you would rather place the bundles in a custom location, quit this installer and use the Oedipa.dmg distribution instead — it lets you drag the bundles wherever you want.

In the Customize step you can deselect any plug-in format you do not need (for example, skip CLAP on a Logic-only setup). All three are selected by default.
