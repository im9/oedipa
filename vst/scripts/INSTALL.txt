Oedipa — Tonnetz-based chord exploration MIDI effect
im9 / Free distribution


Installation
------------

Drag Oedipa.component into:
  ~/Library/Audio/Plug-Ins/Components/

Drag Oedipa.vst3 into:
  ~/Library/Audio/Plug-Ins/VST3/

Both folders may need to be created manually if you have no other
plug-ins installed there.


Hosting
-------

Logic Pro          AU MIDI FX slot on a software-instrument track.

Cubase Pro         Load as a VST Instrument and route MIDI out to a
                   downstream software-instrument track. Cubase's MIDI
                   Inserts slot does not accept third-party VST3 by
                   Steinberg policy; the VST3 bundle is categorised as
                   an instrument so it loads on the Instrument track,
                   and the MIDI it emits is routed externally.

Bitwig / Reaper /  AU or VST3 in the host's MIDI fx slot. Best-effort,
Studio One         not formally tested for v1.
